# RiskRadar Datasets

## 2. Environmental 
**1. Global & Intergovernmental Organizations**
- World Bank Open Data (data.worldbank.org)
	- Climate, energy, and environmental indicators by country
- UN Environment Programme (unep.org/resources)
	- Global environmental reports and datasets
- Global Carbon Project (www.globalcarbonproject.org)
	- CO₂ and greenhouse gas emissions data

**2. Climate & Atmosphere**
- Berkeley Earth (berkeleyearth.org/data)
	- Global temperature records and climate data
- Copernicus Climate Change Service (climate.copernicus.eu)
	- European climate monitoring and reanalysis datasets
- CO₂.Earth (co2.earth)
	- Real-time atmospheric CO₂ tracking

**3. Land, Oceans & Biodiversity**
- Global Forest Watch (globalforestwatch.org)
	- Deforestation and forest cover data
- GBIF (Global Biodiversity Information Facility) (gbif.org)
	- Species occurrence and biodiversity datasets
- Ocean Health Index (oceanhealthindex.org)
	- Marine ecosystem health metrics

**4. Air & Water Quality**
- OpenAQ (openaq.org)
	- Open-source, real-time global air quality data (great for APIs!)
- World Resources Institute: Aqueduct (wri.org/aqueduct)
	- Global water risk and stress data
